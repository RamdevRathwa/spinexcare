Our Therapies & Techniques

Chiropractic Adjustments

Precise manual adjustments to align the spine, reduce pain, and improve overall nervous system function.

IASTM (MFR)

Instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization (Myofascial Release) to break down scar tissue and release restrictions.

Cupping Therapy

An ancient technique used to improve blood flow, reduce muscle tightness, and decrease inflammation.

Taping

Application of kinesiology tape to support muscles and joints, reduce pain, and improve function without restricting movement.
