Understanding Spondylosis

Spondylosis is age-related wear and tear affecting the spinal disks and joints, leading to degeneration and pain.

Types and Causes

  • Cervical (neck) spondylosis
  • Thoracic (upper back) spondylosis
  • Lumbar (lower back) spondylosis
  • Age-related changes
  • Occupational factors
  • Previous injuries

Pain Symptoms

  • Neck or back pain
  • Stiffness and reduced mobility
  • Radiating pain to limbs
  • Muscle weakness

Neurological Signs

  • Numbness and tingling
  • Nerve compression symptoms
  • Balance problems
  • Coordination issues

Daily Impact

  • Limited daily activities
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Reduced flexibility
  • Fatigue and discomfort
