Expert care for rotator cuff injuries and shoulder pain, providing relief and restored mobility through specialized chiropractic techniques and rehabilitation.

Understanding Rotator Cuff Injuries

The rotator cuff consists of four muscles and their tendons that stabilize your shoulder joint. Injury to these structures can significantly impact daily activities.

About Rotator Cuff Injuries

Rotator cuff injuries can range from mild strains to complete tears, affecting shoulder movement and strength.

  • Acute injuries from sudden movements or falls
  • Repetitive overhead activities
  • Age-related degeneration
  • Sports and work-related injuries
  • Poor posture and muscle imbalances

Common Symptoms

Recognize the signs of rotator cuff injury and shoulder problems

Pain Patterns

  • Deep ache in the shoulder
  • Pain when lifting arm
  • Night pain when lying on affected side
  • Radiating pain down the arm

Movement Issues

  • Weakness when lifting or rotating arm
  • Reduced range of motion
  • Difficulty with overhead activities
  • Clicking or catching sensations

Daily Impact

  • Trouble sleeping on affected side
  • Difficulty with daily tasks
  • Weakness when lifting objects
  • Increased pain with activity
