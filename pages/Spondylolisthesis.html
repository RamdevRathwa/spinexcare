Understanding Spondylolisthesis

A condition where one vertebra slips forward over the vertebra below it, causing pain and instability.

Types and Causes

  • Degenerative - Age-related wear
  • Isthmic - Stress fractures
  • Congenital - Present at birth
  • Traumatic - Injury related
  • Sports activities impact

Back Symptoms

  • Lower back pain
  • Muscle tightness
  • Stiffness
  • Limited mobility

Nerve Symptoms

  • Leg pain or sciatica
  • Numbness/tingling
  • Weakness in legs
  • Balance issues

Activity Impact

  • Walking difficulties
  • Standing problems
  • Exercise limitations
  • Daily task challenges
