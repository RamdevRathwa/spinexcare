Understanding Tennis Elbow

Tennis Elbow is a condition causing pain around the outside of the elbow, typically from overuse of forearm muscles.

Common Causes

  • Repetitive arm movements
  • Sports activities (not just tennis)
  • Work-related tasks
  • Poor technique in activities
  • Muscle imbalances

Common Symptoms

Recognize the signs of Tennis Elbow

Pain Symptoms

  • Outer elbow pain
  • Forearm muscle pain
  • Pain when lifting objects
  • Morning stiffness

Movement Issues

  • Weak grip strength
  • Difficulty extending wrist
  • Pain with daily activities
  • Limited range of motion

Impact on Activities

  • Difficulty with sports
  • Work performance issues
  • Challenges with lifting
  • Affected daily tasks
