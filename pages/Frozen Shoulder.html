Understanding Frozen Shoulder

Frozen Shoulder develops gradually, causing pain and stiffness in the shoulder joint.

Freezing Stage

  • Gradual onset of pain
  • Increasing stiffness
  • Reduced range of motion
  • Duration: 6-9 months

Frozen Stage

  • Reduced pain
  • Significant stiffness
  • Limited movement
  • Duration: 4-12 months

Thawing Stage

  • Gradual improvement
  • Increasing mobility
  • Decreasing stiffness
  • Duration: 6-12 months
