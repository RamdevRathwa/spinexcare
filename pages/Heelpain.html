Understanding Heel Pain

Heel pain can significantly impact your daily activities and quality of life. Understanding its causes is key to effective treatment.

Common Causes of Heel Pain

Heel pain can arise from various conditions and factors that affect the foot's structure and function:

  • Plantar Fasciitis - inflammation of the foot's connective tissue
  • Heel Spurs - calcium deposits on the underside of the heel bone
  • Achilles Tendinitis - inflammation of the Achilles tendon
  • Excessive Pronation - improper foot mechanics
  • Overuse or sudden increase in activity
