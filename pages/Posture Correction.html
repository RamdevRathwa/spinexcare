Understanding Posture Correction

Good posture is essential for optimal health and well-being, affecting everything from spine health to organ function.

About Posture Correction

Poor posture can develop from various lifestyle factors and habits, leading to numerous health issues if left uncorrected.

  • Digital device usage and desk work
  • Muscular imbalances and weakness
  • Previous injuries or conditions
  • Lifestyle and occupational habits
  • Stress and emotional factors
  • Improper ergonomics

Physical Signs

  • Forward head posture
  • Rounded shoulders
  • Uneven hips or shoulders

Pain Symptoms

  • Headaches
  • Joint stiffness
  • Muscle fatigue

Related Issues

  • Reduced flexibility
  • Breathing difficulties
  • Digestive problems
  • Poor balance
