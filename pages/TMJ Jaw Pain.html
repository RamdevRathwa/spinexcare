About TMJ Disorders

TMJ disorders affect the jaw joint and surrounding muscles, causing pain and dysfunction in jaw movement.

  • Teeth grinding (bruxism)
  • Jaw misalignment
  • Stress and tension
  • Arthritis
  • Injury or trauma

Common Symptoms

Jaw Symptoms

Pain or tenderness in jaw, clicking sounds, difficulty chewing

Related Pain

Facial pain, ear pain, temple headaches

Movement Issues

Limited jaw movement, locked jaw, difficulty opening mouth
