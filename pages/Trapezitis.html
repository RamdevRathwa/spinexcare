About Trapezitis

Trapezitis is inflammation of the trapezius muscle, causing pain and stiffness in the upper back, neck, and shoulders.

  • Poor posture and desk work
  • Muscle strain from overuse
  • Stress and tension
  • Carrying heavy bags
  • Sleep position issues

Common Symptoms

[Add a short symptoms intro]

Pain Patterns

Neck and shoulder pain, stiffness in upper back, and muscle knots

Movement Issues

Reduced neck mobility, difficulty turning head, and shoulder tension

Associated Symptoms

Headaches, fatigue, and radiating pain to arms
