Understanding Sciatica

Sciatica occurs when the sciatic nerve, which runs from your lower back through your hips and down each leg, becomes compressed or irritated.

About Sciatica

Sciatica is not a condition itself, but a symptom of an underlying problem. Understanding its causes is crucial for effective treatment.

  • Herniated or bulging discs
  • Spinal stenosis
  • Piriformis syndrome
  • Pregnancy-related compression
  • Poor posture and sitting habits
  • Degenerative disc disease

Common Symptoms

Recognize the signs of sciatica for early intervention and better treatment outcomes.

Pain Patterns

  • Lower back pain
  • Radiating leg pain
  • Hip discomfort
  • Buttock pain

Sensory Changes

  • Numbness or tingling
  • Burning sensation
  • Pins and needles feeling
  • Muscle weakness

Impact on Activities

  • Difficulty sitting
  • Pain when walking
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Limited mobility
