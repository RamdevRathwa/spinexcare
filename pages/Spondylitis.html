Understanding Spondylitis

Spondylitis is inflammation of the vertebrae that can lead to severe pain and stiffness in the spine.

Types and Characteristics

  • Ankylosing Spondylitis
  • Non-radiographic axial spondyloarthritis
  • Reactive Spondylitis
  • Early-onset symptoms
  • Progressive nature

Common Symptoms

  • Lower back pain
  • Morning stiffness
  • Reduced flexibility
  • Fatigue

Related Issues

  • Hip pain
  • Neck pain
  • Joint inflammation
  • Poor posture

Impact on Life

  • Sleep disturbances
  • Work limitations
  • Activity restrictions
  • Quality of life effects
