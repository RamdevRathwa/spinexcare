Understanding Plantar Fasciitis

Plantar fasciitis is inflammation of the thick band of tissue that connects your heel bone to your toes, causing significant foot pain and discomfort.

About Plantar Fasciitis

This common condition affects the plantar fascia, causing pain and inflammation that can significantly impact daily activities.

  • Most common cause of heel pain
  • Affects both athletes and non-athletes
  • Can become chronic if untreated
  • Often worse in the morning
  • May affect one or both feet
  • Treatable through proper care
