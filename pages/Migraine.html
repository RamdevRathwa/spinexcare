Understanding Migraines

Migraines are more than just headaches - they're complex neurological conditions that can significantly impact daily life.

About Migraines

Migraines can be triggered by various factors and often involve multiple symptoms beyond head pain.

  • Intense, throbbing head pain
  • Sensitivity to light and sound
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Visual disturbances (aura)
  • Neck tension and stiffness
  • Duration of 4-72 hours

Common Symptoms

Recognize the signs and phases of migraine attacks

Pre-Attack (Prodrome)

  • Food cravings
  • Mood changes
  • Neck stiffness
  • Fatigue

During Attack

  • Severe head pain
  • Sensitivity to stimuli
  • Nausea/vomiting
  • Dizziness

Post-Attack

  • Fatigue
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Mild head pain
  • Neck tension
