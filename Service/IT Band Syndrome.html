Understanding IT Band Syndrome

IT Band Syndrome occurs when the iliotibial band, a thick band of tissue running from your hip to your knee, becomes tight or inflamed.

What Causes IT Band Syndrome?

  • Overuse from running or cycling
  • Poor biomechanics during exercise
  • Muscle imbalances in hips and legs
  • Sudden increases in training intensity
  • Inadequate warm-up or recovery

Common Symptoms

Recognize the signs of IT Band Syndrome

Lateral Knee Pain

Sharp or burning pain on the outside of the knee, especially during running or cycling.

Hip Discomfort

Pain or tightness at the hip joint, particularly during activity.

Activity-Related Pain

Symptoms that worsen with repetitive activities and downhill running.
