Understanding SI-HIP Joint Pain

The sacroiliac (SI) joint connects your pelvis to your spine, while hip joints connect your leg bones to your pelvis. Both are crucial for movement and stability.

Common Causes

  • Poor posture or alignment
  • Pregnancy and childbirth
  • Sports injuries
  • Arthritis or degeneration
  • Muscle imbalances
  • Leg length differences

Common Symptoms

Recognize the signs of SI joint and hip dysfunction

Pain Patterns

  • Lower back pain
  • Hip pain
  • Groin discomfort
  • Buttock pain

Movement Issues

  • Difficulty walking
  • Pain when standing
  • Stiffness
  • Limited mobility

Associated Symptoms

  • Muscle tension
  • Radiating pain
  • Balance problems
  • Sleep disturbances
