Understanding Lordosis

Lordosis is an excessive inward curve of the lower back that can cause postural problems and pain.

About Lordosis

While some degree of lordotic curve is normal, excessive lordosis can lead to various complications and discomfort. Common causes include:

  • Poor posture and muscle imbalances
  • Obesity and pregnancy
  • Spinal conditions and injuries
  • Hip joint problems
  • Genetic factors

Physical Signs

Pain Symptoms

  • Hip and leg discomfort
  • Limited flexibility

Related Issues

  • Poor balance
