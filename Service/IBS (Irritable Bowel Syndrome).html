Understanding IBS

Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS) is a common functional disorder affecting the digestive system, causing various uncomfortable symptoms that can impact daily life.

What is IBS?

IBS is a chronic condition that affects the large intestine, causing various digestive symptoms. While the exact cause isn't fully understood, several factors can contribute to its development:

  • Nervous system dysfunction
  • Muscle contractions in the intestine
  • Inflammation in the intestines
  • Severe infection
  • Changes in gut bacteria
  • Stress and anxiety
