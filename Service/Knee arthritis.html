Understanding Knee Arthritis

Knee arthritis is a degenerative condition that affects the knee joint, causing pain, stiffness, and reduced mobility.

Types and Causes

There are several types of knee arthritis, with osteoarthritis being the most common. Understanding the condition is key to effective treatment.

  • Osteoarthritis - wear and tear of joint cartilage
  • Rheumatoid arthritis - autoimmune inflammation
  • Post-traumatic arthritis - following injury
  • Age-related degeneration
  • Previous knee injuries or surgeries
  • Genetic factors and obesity

Pain and Stiffness
