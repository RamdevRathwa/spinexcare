Problems We Treat

Back Pain

Back pain is a common ailment that can stem from various causes, including muscle strains, injuries, or structural issues in the spine.

NeckPain

is a common condition that can range from a mild annoyance to a debilitating pain.

Cervical/Lumbar Disc Treatment

Cervical and lumbar pain, affecting the neck and lower back respectively, are common musculoskeletal issues with various potential causes

Cervical Spondylosis

Cervical spondylosis, also known as neck arthritis, is a common, age-related condition characterized by the degeneration of discs and joints in the neck (cervical spine).

Sciatica

Sciatica pain refers to pain that radiates along the sciatic nerve, which runs from the lower back down the back of each leg

Herinated Disc

A herniated disc, also known as a slipped or ruptured disc, occurs when the soft, jelly-like center of a spinal disc pushes out through a tear in the tough exterior, potentially irritating nearby nerves and causing pain, numbness, or weakness

Heel Pain

Heel pain is a common ailment with various potential causes, the most frequent being plantar fasciitis, an inflammation of the tissue connecting the heel to the toes

Knee Arthritis/Pain

Knee arthritis refers to inflammation and deterioration of the knee joint, primarily the cartilage

Frozen Shoulder

Frozen shoulder, also known as adhesive capsulitis, is a condition characterized by pain and stiffness in the shoulder joint, limiting its range of motion

Posture Correction

Improve your body alignment and reduce strain by correcting poor posture habits with personalized therapy.

TMJ / Jaw Pain

Effective treatment for jaw tension, TMJ disorders, and facial pain to restore smooth jaw function.

Sports Injury Rehab

Recovery programs for ligament tears, muscle strains, and sports-related injuries to help you return stronger.

Body Stiffness Relief

Loosen up tight muscles and improve joint flexibility to move freely and comfortably again.

Headache & Migraine Relief

Reduce frequency and intensity of migraines and chronic headaches with safe, non-invasive therapies.

Tennis Elbow

Treat pain and inflammation from repetitive elbow use with targeted exercises and soft tissue work.

Carpal Tunnel Syndrome

Alleviate wrist pain, tingling, and numbness with gentle mobilization and ergonomic corrections.

Plantar Fasciitis

Specialized therapy for heel pain and inflammation of the plantar fascia to restore foot comfort and mobility.

SI-HIP Joint Pain

Effective treatment for sacroiliac and hip joint dysfunction, relieving pain and improving pelvic alignment.

Body Alignment

Correcting postural imbalances by aligning the spine, shoulders, hips, and knees for optimal body mechanics and movement.

Spondylosis

Non-surgical treatment for age-related spinal wear and tear, reducing stiffness and chronic neck or back pain.

Spondylitis

Targeted therapy for spinal inflammation, improving flexibility and reducing discomfort caused by spondylitis.

Lordosis

Correction and management of excessive inward spinal curvature (lordosis) to improve posture and core stability.

Spondylolisthesis

Therapy and stabilization for vertebral slippage, helping to reduce back pain and improve spinal alignment.

Kyphosis

Treatment for rounded upper back (kyphosis), focused on posture correction and spinal strengthening.

Scoliosis

Non-surgical scoliosis care, including exercise-based correction and posture training for spinal balance.

Therapies & Techniques

Chiropractic

Chiropractic care can help you find relief from back pain, neck pain, headaches, and other musculoskeletal issues, allowing you to live a healthier, more active life.

Musculoskeletal Assestment and Treatment

Musculoskeletal assessment involves evaluating the body's muscles, bones, joints, and related soft tissues to identify impairments and functional limitations

Pain Management

Comprehensive approach to relieve chronic pain in the back, neck, joints, and muscles using modern techniques.

Tapping

Taping provides support to muscles and joints without restricting movement.

Cupping Therapy

Cupping uses suction to improve blood flow, release muscle tightness, and reduce inflammation.

Dry needling involves inserting thin, sterile needles into trigger points (tight knots in muscles) to release tension, reduce pain, and improve movement.
